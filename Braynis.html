Braynis

“Braynis” or commonly titled “Old Man Braynis” Is A Very Old Species Of Bronis. He is expected to be older than 100 years old.

First Sighting of Species: 1920s, But the Bronis Archive staff re-discovered him and archived him in 2021.

Last Sighting of Species: 12/20/24

The Origin Of Braynis Is Unknown.
