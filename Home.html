The Bronis Archive

This is a site dedicated to the history and archival of the strange thing referred to as a "Bronis."

What is a Bronis?

A Bronis is a very rare species, seemingly a human hybrid. All that we know about Bronis is that he had a doctor's appointment.

History of the Bronis

The first Bronis we archived was found in 2020, since then we have been finding different species of Bronis dating back to over 100 years ago.

About

The Bronis Archive is made and ran by multiple anonymous people.
